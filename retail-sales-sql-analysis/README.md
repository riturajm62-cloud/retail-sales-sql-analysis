# 📊 Retail Sales SQL Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data using SQL to identify key business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends.

The analysis focuses on understanding how different factors such as discount, region, product category, and customer contribution impact overall business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing regions and categories
- Understand the impact of discount on profitability
- Evaluate customer contribution and behavior
- Analyze regional efficiency and management performance
- Study monthly sales trends

---

## 🗂️ Dataset Information
The dataset used is based on a Global Superstore / Retail Sales dataset.

It contains:
- Order-level sales data
- Customer and product details
- Regional and market information
- Profit, discount, and shipping cost

The dataset was cleaned and structured before analysis.

Source: Kaggle, Public dataset (Superstore Sales dataset)

## 📈 Key Insights
- High discounts (>50%) significantly reduce profitability and lead to losses.
- Central region generates the highest revenue but operates at a lower margin.
- North Asia shows the best balance between sales and profitability.
- Southeast Asia has high sales but extremely low profit margins (~2%).
- Some high-value customers generate negative profit.
- Pricing strategy plays a more critical role than shipping cost in profitability.

👉 For detailed insights, refer to **insights.md**

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Reduce excessive discounting to improve profitability
- Focus growth on high-performing regions like North Asia
- Improve pricing strategy for loss-making products and customers
- Assign management to underperforming regions
- Optimize discount strategies for better margins
